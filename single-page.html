How is Envision E-cell a change factor in IIM BG?

Written by Piyush Pallav IIM BODHGAYA E-cell Commitee Member

We 'Envision E-Cell' the entrepreneurship and industry relations cell of IIM Bodhgaya aim to provide an estrade through which students can access entrepreneurial know how, resources, network, and share innovative ideas. The cell is dedicated to act as a catalyst in facilitating emergence of competent entrepreneurial aspirations to the students in order to promote the entrepreneurship and start-up culture. Our objective is to act as a conduit for budding entrepreneurs in building a healthy environment where they can groom their business skills. The cell not only promotes entrepreneurial spirit but also help chisel our intrapreneurial component. The ability to conceptualise ideas and work out different ways alongside helping start-ups to combat difficulties coming their way.

From the last couple of years E-Cell has created its unique brand value on a pan-India scale with a plethora of initiatives taken up and event conducted along the line of our vision to empower entrepreneurship. Some of the front-runner in these programs include ‘Youth Entrepreneurship Summit’ (YES) which is also the flagship event of Envision cell. It envisages fostering entrepreneurial initiatives and activities in IIM Bodhgaya. YES aims to provides a platform for the ‘aspiring entrepreneurs’ to have hands-on experience before they can finally enter the market. With a prize money of Rupees Two lakh fifty thousand, YES'2020 was a national level mega event in which start-ups from PAN India participated.The two days of YES'20 provided networking, mentorship workshop and a platform to compete against the best start-ups ideas nationwide.

‘Summers in Start-up’ is another successful program of Envision E-Cell in which student are provided the hands-on experience and an exposure to the working set-up of start-ups through live projects and internships.

‘Entrepreneurs Talk series’ initiative of Envision E-Cell features founders of Start-ups which are changing the face of Entrepreneurship in our country by coming up new solutions to the existing problems. The founders share their experiences as well as the challenges faced by them while building their Start-up. Till date, we have hosted Mr. Amit Kumar Agarwal (CEO & Founder - NoBroker.com), Mr. Anil G (Co-founder, and COO, Bounce) and Ms. Lakshmi Chaudhry (Founder of Splainer Media). These interactive, informative, and thought-provoking sessions expose students to the ground realities of entrepreneurship and inculcate entrepreneurial flair in them.

Besides the cell also serves as a conduit for young entrepreneurs of IIM Bodh Gaya to get mentorship as well as capital support and consultancy, we facilitate the in-house start-ups at IIM Bodh Gaya by connecting them with VC’s, seed and angel investor’s, accelerators etc. We have also nurtured a number of start-ups by assisting them from ideation to pitching in front of incubators and investors. Some of the start-ups that have been supported by Envision cell during the due course of last one year include Homemade, My3do, and Daily Milk etc. In the upcoming days, we are also planning to start our own Incubation Centre. We have already entered into the implementation phase of Incubation Cell.

The zeal of Envision E-Cell to take IIM Bodhgaya to the next level of success can be demonstrated through its involvement in hosting first ever TEDx and Model United Nations (MUN) events. It keeps collaborating with internal and external clubs, committees and organisations for the development of IIM Bodh Gaya. Envision E-Cell of IIM Bodh Gaya is on a mission to metamorphose IIM Bodhgaya into a premier and productive resource centre for the diffusion of entrepreneurial learning. It aims to contribute to the development of a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem in Bihar and the nation that is inclusive of innovation, enterprise, sustainability, and skill expansion.

Regards.
Envision Cell – IIM Bodh Gaya.
